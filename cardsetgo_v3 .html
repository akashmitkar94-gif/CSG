Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to apply for an HDFC credit card? +
The minimum age requirement for all HDFC credit cards is 21 years. For salaried applicants, the maximum age is 60 years, while for self-employed individuals it is 65 years. You must be an Indian national to apply.
Is the Pixel Play card really Lifetime Free? +
Yes! HDFC Pixel Play is currently available as a Lifetime Free card (₹0 joining and ₹0 annual fee) via the PayZapp app. This is a limited period offer — apply as early as possible to lock in the LTF benefit.
Is Card Set Go affiliated with HDFC Bank? +
No. Card Set Go is a completely independent advisory service and is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or a representative of HDFC Bank. We are a third-party referral platform that helps you choose and apply for the right card.
Do I need to pay any fee to Card Set Go? +
Absolutely not. Our advisory and application guidance service is 100% free for you. We earn a referral commission from HDFC Bank upon successful card issuance — this does not affect the advice or card you receive.
What documents do I need to apply? +
Typically: PAN Card, Aadhaar (address proof), latest 3-month salary slips (or ITR for self-employed), and 3-month bank statement. Exact requirements vary by card. Akash will guide you through the complete document checklist on WhatsApp.
Which card is best for beginners with no credit history? +
The HDFC Pixel Play (Lifetime Free) is an excellent first card — it requires no annual fee and can be obtained against a Fixed Deposit (FD-backed) if you have no credit history. Chat with Akash to find the best starting option for you.
How long does approval take? +
HDFC Bank typically processes applications within 7–15 working days. Digital-first cards like Pixel Play can be issued almost instantly via the PayZapp app. Physical cards are delivered to your address within 5–7 days after approval.
Can I apply if I'm self-employed? +
Yes! Most HDFC credit cards are available to self-employed individuals. Income eligibility is assessed based on your ITR (Income Tax Return). Each card has a different minimum ITR requirement — Akash can help identify the best card for your profile.
